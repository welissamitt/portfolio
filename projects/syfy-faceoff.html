Syfy - Face Off

Employer: Clickfire Media
Role: Design, ui/ux

The goal of the app was to create an interactive environment where fans could see the step-by-step process of how the make-up artists created some of their characters. In addition, the 'Monster Me' feature allowed fans to upload a photo of themselves and see how they'd look with all of the makeup applied.

Basic direction was given from the client. Wireframes were created, and then visual design followed.

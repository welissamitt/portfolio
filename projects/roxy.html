Roxy - Dare Yourself

Employer: Clickfire Media
Role: Design, ui/ux

The #Dare Yourself campaign was created, inviting young women around the world between the ages of 18-28 to submit photo and video content answering why they should be the new face of Roxy using the call-to-action #roxydares.

Using the wireframes created by our team, the visual design then captured what it means to be a Roxy girl, and provided a platform to share what being a Roxy girl is all about!

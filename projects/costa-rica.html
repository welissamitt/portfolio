Go Costa Rica

Employer: Clickfire Media
Role: Design

We needed to produce a realistic map showcasing Costa Rica's popular tourist attractions and lush, beautiful landscape.

Popular landmarks' scale was exagerrated to provide better details on the map. Users of the app can choose a landmark to get a closer look as well as a detailed description.

This project combines photos from the client with detailed matte painting.
